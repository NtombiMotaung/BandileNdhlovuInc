OUR SERVICES

These are the services that we offer

Conveyancing and Notary

We provide comprehensive conveyancing services for property transactions, ensuring compliance with legal requirements and smooth transfer of property ownership. Our notarial services include the certification of documents, legalizations, and drafting of notarial deeds.

Civil and Criminal Litigation

Our firm offers robust representation in both civil and criminal matters. We handle disputes involving personal injury, property claims, contract disputes, and criminal defense, employing strategic litigation techniques to achieve favorable outcomes for our clients.

Divorce and Family Law

We offer expert legal assistance in divorce proceedings, child custody issues, alimony, and other family law matters. Our approach emphasizes sensitivity and thoroughness to support our clients through complex and emotionally charged situations.

Labour Law

Our services encompass all aspects of labour law, including employment disputes, unfair dismissal claims, and workplace harassment. We provide advice and representation to both employers and employees, aiming to resolve conflicts efficiently and equitably.

Contract Drafting and Management

We specialize in drafting and managing contracts to ensure clarity, enforceability, and alignment with our clients' objectives. Our services include reviewing existing agreements and advising on contract compliance and risk management.

Estate Planning and Administration

Our firm assists clients with estate planning to ensure the effective management and distribution of assets. We provide comprehensive services in estate administration, including the preparation of wills, trusts, and handling of probate matters.

Mediation and Arbitration:

We offer mediation and arbitration services as alternative dispute resolution methods, helping parties reach amicable settlements outside of traditional court proceedings. Our focus is on facilitating effective communication and finding mutually agreeable solutions.

Medical Negligence

We provide expert legal representation in cases of medical negligence, advocating for patients who have suffered due to substandard medical care. Our firm utilizes our deep understanding of healthcare to build strong cases and secure just compensation for affected individuals.

Road Accident Fund (RAF) Claims:

Our firm offers specialized services in handling Road Accident Fund (RAF) claims. We assist clients in navigating the complexities of the RAF process to secure fair compensation for injuries sustained in road accidents. Our expertise ensures that our clients' claims are presented with precision and diligence, maximizing their chances of a successful outcome.
